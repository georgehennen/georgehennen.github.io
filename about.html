About Me

With a background as a M.S. in chemical engineering, I gained a strong foundation in the principles and practices of the chemical engineering. More importantly, my engineering background provided me with the knowledge on how to solve problems systematically. My educational background instilled in me a passion for finding creative solutions to complex problems, and an eagerness to learn and grow.

Recently, I have been lucky enough to join a rotational program in the CDMO (Contract Development and Manufacturing Organization) space. Through this program, I have been able to gain valuable experience in a variety of different business fucntions, including process development, capital project engineering, financial planning & analysis, business analysis, and project management.

While I have enjoyed my time in the CDMO space, I have recently developed a strong interest in data & business analytics, and have placed in a role as a business analyst. I am excited to grow my career in this field and to use my analytical skills to help organizations make better, more informed decisions.
